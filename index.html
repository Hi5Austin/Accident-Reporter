Where did you get hit?

Enter the address where you got hit in the textbox below and click start twice.

Trace the path you were walking with the line tool.

(No need to click and drag. Click the line tool, then just click the places you passed.)

After that, click on the marker tool.

Then leave markers with the marker tools on the intersections that you passed.

Make sure to place the markers in the order you passed the intersections.

Click reset if you made a mistake when placing the markers.

Click finish when you're done.
